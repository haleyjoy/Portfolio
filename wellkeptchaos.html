Well Kept Chaos

Spot Illustrations
Designed for Catherine Sarlis of http://www.wellkeptchaos.com/



The Project

Catherine was working on rebranding her party planning and lifestyle blog, Well Kept Chaos. She wanted a set of sticker-style spot illustrations that she can use throughout her blog and social media.

The Solution

Catherine's blog is full of bright colors, and lots of fun. I wanted to capture that spirit and create some spot illustrations that would certainly correlate with what she regularly blogs about. Initially I went with greys where the blues are currently, and Catherine requested I change the greys to various shades of blue.
The result is a set of bright, cheerful, and fun illustrations that will supplement her blog posts, Facebook posts, and Instagram posts.
