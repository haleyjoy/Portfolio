Loco Moda

Fashion is prevalent in all of our lives. We’ve perhaps mastered the day-to-day, we know what to wear to work, or on the weekend when we relax. But when it comes to those special events, whether it’s a music festival, a renaissance faire, a themed party, or a cosplay event- shopping at the mall just doesn’t cut it. Loco Moda is a community of fashion lovers, creatives, artists, and designers, where shoppers looking for the perfect piece can find one-of-a-kind wearable art.

Street Sweet

In recent years, Generation X’ers and Millenials have flocked to cities like New York, San Francisco, Los Angeles, etc, particularly due to the growth of the tech industry / start ups. This clustering of young, trendy, work-minded individuals gave birth to another trend: Food Trucks. Having experienced convenience-based dining in San Francisco, and admittedly having a notable sweet tooth, I designed Street Sweet, an app that helps Food Truck lovers find and order from their favorite sweet-themed Food Trucks.
