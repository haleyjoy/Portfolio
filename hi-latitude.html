Hi, Latitude!


I spent an embarrassing amount of time poring over the genius that makes up the work boasted on the Latitude website. “Geeking out” is probably putting it gently.

You guys are clearly experts at connecting consumers with brands in a way that sticks. By encouraging beauty bloggers at the Ulta booth at BlogHer 2013 to record and share their stories, the “advertisement” becomes not only a memorable experience for participants, but also forms a personal bond between the Ulta brand and each blogger. Even your website’s 90 second “About” video immediately has the viewer identify as the vermillion-colored dot, and just as quickly aligns Latitude by referencing "an agency" as well as your services in the same shade. Within 90 seconds, the seed has been planted that by hiring your agency, a company can propel its brand above the rest and reach consumers in ways it hasn't before.

I am a high-energy person who thrives in the company of other make-it-happen people. Yes, I have skills: UX/UI, Illustration, Visual Storytelling, Graphic Design and CNC Laser Engraving to name a few. But more importantly, I have creative talent and a mind that thinks out-of-the-box.  I would be thrilled to put these skills and talents to work at Latitude, where new challenges are embraced with enthusiasm and resourcefulness.

Most of the work in my Portfolio is UX/UI focused. I most recently finished working on Happy Hour, an app design that allows users to record wines and beers they have enjoyed, discover new bottles, and share their wine and beer wishlist with family and friends. I conducted user research, created user stories and user flows, designed wireframes, and fleshed out the finished HD screens, which I used to create a clickable prototype. I also designed Loco Moda, a buying and selling platform for fashion lovers and creatives, where shoppers can find one-of-a-kind wearable art. To see these projects and more of the stuff I've worked on, head over to my Portfolio.

Regardless of whether you are hiring right now, I would love the opportunity to sit down and make a personal connection with someone from Latitude and hear more about the awesome work you guys are doing.

Looking forward to hearing from you!

Sincerely,
Haley Guilfoile
